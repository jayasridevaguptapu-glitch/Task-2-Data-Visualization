# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective

To create meaningful visualizations from sales data and derive business insights using Power BI.

## Tool Used

* Power BI Desktop

## Dataset

* Superstore Sales Dataset

## Dashboard Components

1. Total Sales KPI
2. Total Profit KPI
3. Total Quantity KPI
4. Sales by Category
5. Profit by Category
6. Sales by Region
7. Monthly Sales Trend
8. Sales by Segment
9. Top 10 States by Sales

## Key Business Insights

* Technology category generated the highest sales and profit.
* Consumer segment contributed the largest share of revenue.
* West region showed strong sales performance.
* Sales exhibited seasonal trends throughout the year.
* A small number of states accounted for a large portion of total sales.
* Profitability varied across categories, indicating opportunities for business optimization.

## Outcome

Successfully developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that communicates sales performance through effective visual storytelling and business insights.
